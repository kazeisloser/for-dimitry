To My Dearest

DIMITRY

For the one with whom i want to succeed. the one whom i want to build a future with. i have a small name i use, one that might sound strange to others “Dimitry”. not something carefully chosen, just a name that felt right, the moment Rafli became a quiet place to return to. this website isnt a monument, dont worry... im not counting days with a red marker on the calendar. its just a small space for things that dont fit neatly in my head, but feel to precious to forget. we've known each other for quite some time, yes 2 years, but lets just say “around that,” so it doesnt look like im the only one keeping track. Long enough to know which habits are annoying, and which ones secretly turn into longing. Here, you'll find simple things: game moments, unplanned photos, and feelings that grew without ever asking to be named. i made this website not because the story is grand, but because its real. because Dimitry has been someone behind the screen, beside me, and in ordinary days that quietly became worth remembering. if one day you read this and smile just a little, then this website has done its job. because its purpose is simple, to keep a story, without making it feel heavy. and if someone ever asks, “Why Dimitry?” ill probably answer casually: “i dont know. it just feels right, like you.”
